Home of the One Gallon Wash.

Your choice in Albuquerque for eco-friendly, professional, and insured automotive detailing; from maintenance washes to ceramic coating. Living in a desert we are constantly in a drought. At Steam Dream Auto Detailing we use the power of steam to cut water consumption to only two gallons while providing a scratch free surface.
